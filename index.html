FAQ

How do I add the bot?

Click Invite at the top, select a server, and grant the requested permissions. Then run /setchannels and /verify setup to get going.

Why don’t I see moderation commands?

Only members with the required Discord permissions (Kick/Ban/Moderate/Manage Messages) will see those commands. The help list hides items you can’t use.

Is Lyrifall free?

Yes — core features are free. You can self‑host or use our managed instance.
